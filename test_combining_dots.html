Combining Character Dot Centering Test

Base digits (no dots):
1 2 3 4 5 6 7
With U+0307 COMBINING DOT ABOVE:
1̇ 2̇ 3̇ 4̇ 5̇ 6̇ 7̇
With U+0323 COMBINING DOT BELOW:
1̣ 2̣ 3̣ 4̣ 5̣ 6̣ 7̣
Sharp + combining dot above (1# with dot):
1♯̇ 2♯̇ 3♯̇

If dots are NOT centered over the base digit, the test fails.
The dot should appear directly above/below the CENTER of each digit.
